Camera Controls:

  • Use the arrow pad to rotate the camera
  • Use W - S to move forward and back
  • Use A - D to move left and right
  • Use Q - E to move up and down

    • Animations Avaliable:

  • - Press 'J' to open and close all the doors
  • - Press 'K' to make the fire alarm look active!
  • - Press 'L' to see a lighting animation
